Basketball

Description:Basketball is a team sport played between two teams, usually consisting of five players each, on a rectangular court12345. The objective is to get the ball through a hoop mounted high on a backboard on the opponent's side of the court, while preventing the opponent from shooting it into your team's hoop1345. A goal is worth two points, three if shot from outside a specified limit .

VolleyBall

Description:Volleyball is a team sport played by two teams, usually of six players on a side. The players use their hands to bat a ball back and forth over a high net, trying to make the ball touch the court within the opponents’ playing area before it can be returned. Volleyball has been part of the official program of the Summer Olympic Games since Tokyo 1964.

FootBall

Description:Football, also called soccer, is a game played by two teams of 11 players each. Players use any part of their bodies except their hands and arms to maneuver the ball into the opposing team’s goal. Only the goalkeeper is permitted to handle the ball and may do so only within the penalty area surrounding the goal. The aim of football is to score more goals than the opponent in a 90-minute playing time frame. The match is split into two halves of 45 minutes each, with a 15-minute halftime rest period.

ThrowBall

Description:Throwball is played between two teams, each with seven players on the court at a time. The court is divided into two halves by a net, and the objective is to score points by successfully throwing the ball over the net into the opponent’s court. A team can hit the ball a maximum of three times to return it to the other side.

Each player starts with sixteen pieces, including a king, queen, rooks, bishops, knights, and pawns. The objective is to checkmate the opponent's king by placing it in a position where it cannot escape capture123. The game involves no hidden information or chance elements.
